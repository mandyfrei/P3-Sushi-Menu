Welcome to The Saki House

Welcome to the Saki House! An affordable and contemporary setting to enjoy your favorite sushi menu items. Not only do we provide the best and fresh sushi in the Seattle area, but we also feature some of Seattle's best upcoming musicians by providing live music Friday through Sunday nights.

With our large dinning room and expert sushi chefts, we can provide a quality meal in no time that is tasty and healthy, all while providing awesome entertainment. Be sure to stop by soon, we would absolutely love to have you!
