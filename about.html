How we got started

Have you been looking for great sushi smack dab downtown Seattle? Look no further! The Saki House is your one stop shop which brings you fresh sushi directly to your plate! Whether it is a Tuna Rio roll to the newly designed Mango Roll by our one of a kind Sushi Chefs, your plates will be rolled and cooked right in front of you! There are countless varieties on our menu which we know you and your sushi loving friends will love.

With weekend live music and top shelf cocktails, come to the Saki House for a great time. With the best sushi in town and the best entertainment on the block, the Saki House is the real deal. So bring your friends and family and start eating sushi today!
